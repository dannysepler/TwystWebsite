What is Twyst?

Overview

Our app, simple in nature but sleek in design, is made for performance. We removed the clutter that most music apps have. Instead of opting for a full set of piano keys, or a guitar neck, our app instead has just five buttons. But don't be deceived - with just those buttons, one can play twenty-four different notes, or two full octaves!

Layout

On the right side of the app, there are three buttons that can be used to play a scale. On the left side of the screen, there are two more buttons - one for sharps, and the other for flats. By tilting the phone forward, one plays higher musical notes. And by shaking the phone, a user produces vibrato.

Who is Twyst For?

Twyst was created for musicians, by musicians. We wanted an app that was both easy to hit, but functional enough to bring to a gig. And that's exactly what we got. We think Twyst can make a mark in the musical world. How about you?

What if I don't play music?

If you are not a musician, do not despair! the simplicity of Twyst allows it to also be new-user-friendly. The shaking and twisting of the phone also allows it to be fun for emerging musicians of all ages. Don't believe us? Give it a "Twyst"!
